Why crowdfunding feels broken

Creators and backers are both doing more work than they should.

For creators

  • Winning on traditional platforms often depends on bringing your own audience first.
  • Marketing and maintaining a campaign can feel like a second full-time job.
  • New creators get pushed toward agencies or outside help just to be seen.

For backers

  • Finding fresh campaigns is harder than it should be.
  • Mismanaged campaigns create trust issues and disappointment.
  • The culture often feels more like preordering than supporting new ideas early.

What Quickstarter changes

Short-form discovery meets lightweight funding.

Quickstarter is a micro-crowdfunding platform built around small donations, bite-sized creator content, and immediate rewards. It lowers the barrier for emerging creators while giving backers a more engaging way to discover and support ideas.

Lower lift for creators Launch without running an exhausting full-scale campaign.
Better discovery for backers Find projects through playful, swipeable browsing instead of endless searching.
Reward moments right away Supporters get immediate digital perks that keep the experience exciting.
